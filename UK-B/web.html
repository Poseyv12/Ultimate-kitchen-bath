About Ultimate Kitchen & Bath

Introducing Ultimate Kitchen & Bath, the premier kitchen and bath remodeling business founded by industry veteran Jeff Grossman. With over 25 years of experience in the industry, Jeff has built a reputation for excellence in design, craftsmanship, and customer service. In 2010, Jeff founded Ultimate Kitchen & Bath with a vision to provide homeowners with exceptional design and remodeling services for their kitchens and bathrooms. Since then, Ultimate Kitchen & Bath has become one of the most trusted and sought-after remodeling businesses in the area.

At Ultimate Kitchen & Bath, we believe that a successful remodeling project starts with a strong partnership between our team and our clients. That's why we work closely with each client to understand their unique needs, preferences, and budget, and develop a custom plan to bring their vision to life.

Our team of skilled designers and craftsmen are experts in all aspects of kitchen and bath remodeling, from custom cabinetry and countertops to flooring, lighting, and plumbing. We use only the highest-quality materials and work with trusted suppliers to ensure that every project is executed to perfection.

At Ultimate Kitchen & Bath, we are committed to providing our clients with exceptional service and support every step of the way. From the initial consultation to the final walk-through, we work tirelessly to ensure that every project is completed on time, on budget, and to your complete satisfaction.

If you're ready to transform your kitchen or bath into the ultimate space for your home, contact Ultimate Kitchen & Bath today to schedule a consultation. Our team of experts is here to help you bring your vision to life!
